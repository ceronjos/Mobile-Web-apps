Assignment 1: CleanBot!

Welcome to the world standard of cleanliness. CleanBot is a Zenbo robot that is designed to help us keep your household clean and tidy. Built with state of the art hardware and a friendly integrated smart assistant, CleanBot is built to serve the busy working customer who needs a little extra help keeping the house clean.

Some of the main features of CleanBot include: vaccuuming, air filtertration, air humidification, dusting, counter cleaning, and disinfection.

Smart Assistants include the proprietary assistant called "CleanBot" and other mainstream assistants such as "Google Assistant", "Amazon Alexa", "Siri", and "Bixby".

As for other features, CleanBot also has most other Smart device features such as being able to tell you the weather, perform web searches, set reminders, save alarms, and even tell you the latest news.

We believe that CleanBot will help our society get through these tought times by giving our customers back valuable time and by making you feel confident that your household is free or germs all day long.
